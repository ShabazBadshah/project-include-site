Workshops and bootcamps.

Workshops

"Project Include Workshops are single-session, standalone lessons, perfect for introducing students who are unfamiliar with computer programming to the wonders of coding. Our instructors create curriculum suitable for both elementary and secondary school levels."

Workshops

Bootcamps

"Project Include Bootcamps are available during July and August. These programs run for an extended period with several sessions over the course of a week. Students will learn the fundamentals of computer programming suitable for their age, and can work together with their peers to create their own projects."
